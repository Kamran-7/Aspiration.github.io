OUR PROGRAMS





ASPIRATIONS CLOTHING SUPPORT

ASPIRATIONS reflects its strong and steeped belief in providing a garment of hope where there is nothing but mere frays of hopelessness and helplessness are left! We live in a brutal world where threads of hope are brutally frayed. The fabric that binds us is ripping right from the seams. Sadly, we have lost the realization that we are cut from the same cloth. Yet, in this time of adversity, some roll their sleeves and inspire us with heartwarming deeds. Aspirations trust believes in the garment of hope, not just for those who are lacking the human right of clothing but also who are shunned by the society.

ASPIRATIONS FINANCIAL SUPPORT

ASPIRATIONS trust is creating opportunities to exploit the Islamic principles of interest-free credit (Qard-E-Hassana). Aspirations Islamic Micro financing Scheme dispense needy persons the power to create elbow rooms for themselves and others. Scaled-down entrepreneurs in many underdeveloped nations do not have access to simple banking facilities.





ASPIRATIONS FOOD SUPPORT

Islam is a religion of peace, tranquility, cooperation and brotherhood. Holy Prophet (P.B.U.H) spent his whole life helping the poor and the needy. And instructed his followers to do the same, following the teachings of our beloved Prophet (P.B.U.H) the Aspirations Trust with the assistance of donors is looking after the needs of thousands of families, widows and orphans. For such people every month basic commodities (ration) are provided by Aspirations Trust . Our Food Projects help feed millions of needy people a year from providing low-income families monthly Ration Packs to providing a nutritional and dignified meal twice a day at our Food Kitchen.

ASPIRATIONS EDUCATION SUPPORT

Allocation of children is made according to need by the Sponsorship Department. However, if a sponsor has a preference for a child in a particular project, efforts will be made to comply. Once a child is allocated to a sponsor, a brief history and annual progress reports are provided. The sponsorship scheme is an excellent way for the community to share in the responsibility of providing for these vulnerable children and ensuring that they get a fair chance in life. Sponsors may correspond with their sponsored children and also visit them. Many sponsors establish a close bond with the children who, in turn, derive great benefit from this relationship..
